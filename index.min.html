  • A convenient Weather API that displays the current weather in cities throughout the world as well as a 5 day forecast. Enter a city in the search field, and a full list of weather stats load, temperature, humidity, windspeed, and UVIndex (color coded!).   • In this little quiz app you can test your knowledge of a musical instrument little known to many--the viola! The user first enters his/her initials which are stored in a database via Mongodb. Click the 'Start' button and a multiple choice question loads. Once answered, it disappears, and a new question loads. Your score appears at bottom as a timer counts down-- you have sixty seconds! Once completed, you can submit your final score and the quiz resets.
